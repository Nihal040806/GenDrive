Project Overview

GenDrive is a sophisticated simulation platform that leverages Neuro-Evolutionary Algorithms to train autonomous agents in complex navigation tasks.

Each agent is equipped with proximity sensors and a neural brain that processes spatial data in real-time. Through the process of natural selection, the population progressively masters the art of high-speed navigation.

Core Features

  • Unsupervised Learning:

    Agents discover optimal paths autonomously through trial and error.

  • Real-time Visualization:

    Watch the decision-making process live through our NN visualizer.

  • Scalable Evolution:

    Configure population sizes and mutation rates to optimize learning.
